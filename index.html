ABOUT

I am a physics and computer science teacher based in New York City. I believe wholeheartedly in project-based learning and in increasing access to STEM education, especially for young women and students who have historically been excluded from these fields. As a student at Relay Graduate School of Education, I am currently working towards my Master of Arts in Teaching, where I focus on inclusive and culturally responsive curriculum development.

I completed my undergraduate degree at Wellesley College, graduating in 2020 summa cum laude with a bachelor's degree in physics and a minor in astronomy. While there, I worked in several different labs, jumping around from particle physics to quantum optics to a brief stint in materials science. But my heart always belonged in education. As I learned more and more about the fields I loved, I felt more and more strongly that I wanted to share my love of physics with the next generation.
